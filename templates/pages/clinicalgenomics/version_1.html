miResistantBiome

miResistantBiome (Antibiotic Resistance Determination test) is a sequence based reporting for drug resistance. It determines the presence or absence of established markers for resistance to various drugs.\nNew panel of resistance markers created in-house through data analytics, AI and ML. Targeted therapy recommended by health care professionals based on report will reduce cost and minimize drug side effects.

miImmuneBiome

miImmuneBiome carries out India-specific HLA typing donor match research and applications. HLA are proteins (or markers) found on most cells in the body. The immune system uses these markers to recognize which cells belong to the body and which do not. HLA typing is used in organ transplants like kidney, heart, platelet, liver, lung etc.
