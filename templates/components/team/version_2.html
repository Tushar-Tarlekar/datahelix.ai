LEADERSHIP

The visionary behind our mission of enabling #GenomicsForAll

Dr. Gautam Das, Ph.D.

Co-Founder & CEO, miBiome Therapeutics.

Dr Gautam Das is the Co-Founder & Chief Executive Officer of a genomics company 'miBiome Therapeutics LLP ' specializing in healthcare, pharmaceuticals, agriculture and biotechnology, he is the Chief Scientific Advisor of Microbiome Research Pvt. Ltd .

He has over 17 years of post-Ph.D. experience in research with 19 peer-reviewed high-impact international publications (7 of them from miBiome), with > 1500 citations and 15 granted patents from USA, India, Israel and Australia.

He worked at the R & D division of Reliance Industries Limited (RIL) as Asst. Vice-President and Head of Biomaterials (Synthetic Biology).

He is a Member of Genetics Society of America , USA, Society of Biological Chemists, India and is active in community service, cultural societies and philanthropy. He is a member of Board of Studies committee of NMIMS University, Mumbai and is an industry expert of the ‘Omics’ course of IIT-Jodhpur.

He serves as a member of the Advisory and Monitoring Board of Government-appointed committee and has served as Scientific Consultant for a reputed NGO and as a member at RIL’s Joint Technical Committee and Institutional Bio-Safety Committee.

Ravi Shroff

Co-Founder & Managing Partner, miBiome Therapeutics

A seasoned entrepreneur and business strategist , Ravi co-founded miBiome Therapeutics in 2019 alongside Dr. Gautam Das, combining science driven innovation with strong industry relationships to scale genomic diagnostics across India.

With deep experience spanning multiple startups founding Microbiome Research Pvt. Ltd . (2016), Trudiagno Labs as well as angel investing in four early-stage companies, he brings vision and execution to strategic opportunities.

Together with Dr. Das, Ravi has guided miBiome’s expansion into innovative services from NGS based diagnostics to AI-driven microbiome products impacting clinicians and patients nationwide.
